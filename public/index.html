we've got the answers

What is Google Developer Group?
Google Developer Group (GDG) are community groups for college and university students interested in Google developer technologies. Students can gain knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
How can I join GDG SCOE?
You can join GDG SCOE by clicking the "Join us" button and filling out the membership form. Membership is free and open to all students of SCOE interested in technology and development.
What activities does GDG SCOE organize?
We organize various activities including technical workshops, coding competitions, hackathons, study jams, and project collaborations. We also host speaker sessions with industry experts and networking events.
Do I need to be a computer science student to join?
No, GDG SCOE welcomes students from all academic backgrounds! If you're interested in technology and want to learn more about development, you're welcome to join regardless of your major.
