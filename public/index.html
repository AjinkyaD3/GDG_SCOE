What Is GDG?

Google Developer Groups (GDG) are global communities designed to bring developers together to share knowledge, collaborate, and innovate using Google technologies.

At Google Developer Group SCOE, we create opportunities for students and professionals to collaborate, solve problems, and transform ideas into impactful solutions.

Together, we build the future.
